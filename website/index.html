Popular Commands

Here are some of the most used commands to get you started

Music Controls

.play [song]
Play a song from YouTube, Spotify, or URL
.skip
Skip to the next song in the queue
.stop
Stop playback and clear the queue
.loop
Toggle looping for current song or queue

Queue Management

.queue
View the current music queue
.clear
Clear the current queue
.shuffle
Shuffle the songs in the queue
.remove [position]
Remove a specific song from the queue

Audio Effects

.filter [effect]
Apply audio filters like bassboost, nightcore, etc.
.volume [0-150]
Adjust the playback volume
.ai-dj
Activate AI-powered music recommendations
.clearfilter
Remove all active filters
